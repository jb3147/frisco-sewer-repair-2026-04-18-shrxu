Our Services

From emergency backups to complete sewer line replacements, Metro Flow Plumbing delivers professional, code‑compliant solutions that protect your home, health, and peace of mind—day or night.

Professional Sewer Repair Services in Frisco

Every home is different, but the need for a safe, reliable sewer system is the same. Our licensed plumbers combine advanced diagnostics with proven repair methods to solve problems quickly and permanently.

24/7 Emergency Sewer Repair

When sewage backs up or drains stop working, our emergency team is ready around the clock. We arrive with fully stocked trucks to diagnose and repair urgent sewer issues before they cause costly damage.

Sewer Camera Inspection

Using high‑resolution video cameras, we inspect your sewer line from the inside to locate breaks, roots, bellies, and blockages. This precise diagnosis lets us recommend the most efficient, cost‑effective repair options.

Sewer Line Cleaning & Hydro Jetting

Grease, scale, and debris can slowly choke your sewer line. Our professional cleaning and high‑pressure hydro jetting clear tough clogs and restore proper flow, helping you avoid repeat backups and emergencies.

Trenchless Sewer Repair & Replacement

When pipes are cracked, broken, or collapsed, our trenchless methods repair or replace the line with minimal digging. Preserve your landscaping and driveway while getting a durable, code‑approved sewer solution.

Tree Root Intrusion Solutions

Tree roots seek out moisture and can quickly destroy older sewer lines. We locate and remove root intrusions, repair damaged sections, and recommend long‑term strategies to protect your pipes from future growth.

Sewer Line Replacement & Code Upgrades

For severely deteriorated or undersized systems, we design and install new sewer lines that meet current Frisco and Texas plumbing codes. Our team handles permits, inspections, and clean‑up for a worry‑free project.
